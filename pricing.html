Common Questions
Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?
Yes. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.
What happens to my recordings if I cancel?
Your recordings stay accessible for 30 days after cancellation, giving you time to download them.
What audio format are recordings stored in?
All sets are recorded and stored as 320kbps MP3s — broadcast quality, compact size.
Do you record automatically?
Yes. Once your Twitch, YouTube, Kick, or VRCDN stream goes live, Waxi detects it and starts recording automatically. No manual action needed.
